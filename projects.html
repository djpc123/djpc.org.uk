Projects

A list of some of the projects that I have worked on.

Overheard.co

Overheard is a project that I currently involved in with a bunch of friends, including James Dryden, Rebecca Arnold, Shane Hudson and Rufus Honour, from University. It is inspired by a facebook group where people can post things that they have heard people say, where it is either funny as it has been heard out of context, or a whole conversation. It is still under development and we dont have any idea on a release.

Leftovers

Leftovers was my first mildy finished app that I have made for Android built at AngelHack London in Oct 2012. It is based around the idea of using ingredients to find recipies. James Dryden built a MySQL and PHP based API and Rebecca Arnold created a design and wrote recipies. We are currently finishing it off as it has some rough edges and needs some content.
